    The impacts of modern encryption on society:

    • Encryption secures data under a layer of protection:

    • Previously, symmetrical encryption had issues with the fact that hackers could steal keys.

    • In the modern day however, we have assymetrical encryption which is far more secure.

    • By having a private key to encrypt and decrypt data that you don't send (unlike in symmetrical), it is more secure.

    • Encryption has improved the security of data transfer:

    • This has saved money for the individual consumers transfering important data that if leaked could cost them

    • Not having adequate encryption therefore would add another layer of unecessary stress for the average person

    • But it has also saved money for businesses who don't need to spend as much money on new ways to combat hackers intercepting data

    • Without modern encryption online stores would not be possible for the above reasons.

    • Encryption is super important for militaries in the modern age:

    • Without modern day encryption, military operations could be compromised by even basic militias or terrorists

    • This would either completely stop the modern transfer of sensitive information or put many lives at risk.
